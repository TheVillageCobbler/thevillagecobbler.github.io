About

Looking for the best shoe repair?

The Village cobbler proudly serves Cambridge, Kitchener-Waterloo, Brantford and the surrounding areas on southern Ontario

We are a locally owned and operated shop that has provided expert leather and shoe repairs in Cambridge, Ontario since 1996. Located in the Cambridge Centre mall beside East Side Marios, we have been recognized for our outstanding work by our loyal customers that range all over southern Ontario. We believe no job is too small and no customer should go unheard so make sure to visit or call today if your shoes need repair.

Services


More Than Just Shoe Repair

Along with leather and shoe repair, we offer key cutting, professional knife & scissor sharpening, Handbag repair, Satin party shoes & dyeing, Sports equipment repairs, western boot specialists, shoe stretching and Birkenstock resoling.

We also carry a wide range of high quality products such as cork bags, an extensive line of quality shoe care products, a variety of foot comfort insertsshoe care products, laces, belts and much more.
